AI Meeting Notes — User Guide

Stage 1 Trial

What it is

AI Meeting Notes lets you record conversations at events and automatically receive a clean email summary/transcript. It's lightweight by design: scan a badge → record → get notes by email.

Quick start

  1. Update the app and open the Scans tab. If your event has enabled the feature, an AI Notes drawer opens.
  2. Tap Record sample and grant microphone & background permissions.
  3. Record a short voice sample (10–30s).

You can also start later via Record conversations on the lead capture or profile page.

When you're ready to capture a meeting, tap Record a conversation. Recording starts instantly if you've completed onboarding.

If you don't see AI Notes, the organizer may not have enabled it yet for your event (feature is toggle-controlled).

Onboarding (first time only)

Permissions

You'll be asked to allow microphone and background activity. These are required for recording.

Voice sample

Read the on-screen text for 10–30 seconds (under 10s prompts you to continue; over 30s stops automatically). You can delete and re-record before you submit; after you submit the sample, it can't be changed in the MVP.

Controls while sampling

Pause/Resume, Stop → Preview with Send, Delete, or Close (close shows a confirmation).

Recording a meeting

Start/Stop

Tap Record a conversation. While recording you can Pause/Resume, Stop (then Continue, Delete, or Send), or Close (you'll get a confirmation before exiting). Back actions/swipes also trigger the confirmation.

Works in the background

You'll see a system notification ("Your recording is in progress") and recording continues if the screen is off. If you answer a phone call, recording pauses and resumes when the call ends.

Offline safe

If you're offline, the app saves your recording and sends it when you reconnect. If an upload error occurs, the app lets you know and keeps the recording safe until it can retry.

Where to find your recordings

After scanning an attendee, you'll see which scans have associated recordings in the Scan list and on the lead capture or profile page. Timestamps follow your device time.

What the status means

Uploading… – being sent to the server
Processing… – being transcribed/analyzed
Processed – finished and ready
Failed – something went wrong

Colors indicated in-app help you spot the state at a glance.

Getting your notes

When a recording reaches Processed, both you and the person you scanned automatically receive the transcript/summary by email. Email templates are managed by the organizer, and the language you receive follows your profile settings.

Languages

  • The AI Notes UI and prompts are localized in English and German.
  • The app uses your device language (EN/DE). If your device isn't EN/DE, the UI defaults to English.

Manage & delete recordings

You can delete any meeting recording from the Meeting Recordings list at any status (Uploading/Processing/Processed/Failed). You'll be asked to confirm; once deleted, it's removed from the list and backend storage. This helps meet data-deletion expectations.

Privacy & consent

AI Notes requires a one-time voice sample and explicit permission to record audio and run in the background. You're always in control: start/stop, preview, delete, and send on your terms.

Troubleshooting

I don't see AI Notes

Your event may have it turned off.

Permissions denied

The app will prompt you again; enable microphone & background activity to proceed.

Upload/processing error

The app keeps your recording safe and retries when possible; you'll see a message if something goes wrong.

FAQ

How long should my sample be?

10–30 seconds. Under 10s you'll be asked to continue; over 30s stops automatically.

Can I change my voice sample later?

In the MVP, only before you submit it. After submission, it can't be changed.

Who receives the transcript?

You and the person you scanned, via email, after processing completes.
